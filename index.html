Friends, popular American television sitcom that was created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman and aired on the National Broadcasting Company (NBC) network from 1994 to 2004. It won six Emmy Awards and from its second season until the end of its run maintained a top five or better Nielsen rating.

Friends is about a group of six young adults, Rachel Green, Monica Geller, Phoebe Buffay, Chandler Bing, Joey Tribbiani and Ross Geller.

The finale of Friends was watched by more than 52 million people
